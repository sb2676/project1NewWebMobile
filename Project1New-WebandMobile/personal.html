Some of my hobbies include...

Gaming

I enjoy gaming because of many reasons, but mainly it's because of the stories you get to experience. you get to immerse yourself in all kinds of worlds and stories. I love the diversity of gaming and how it doesn't matter who you are or what you like, you can find a game that you enjoy.

Strolls

I love taking walks, the places where I love to walk the most are beaches, parks, forests, and generally anywhere where it's quiet. It's very calming and gives time to reflect on your day.

Cooking

I enjoy cooking because it's therapeutic for me. You always get to experiment with different ingredients and tastes. I love how in cooking you don't necessarily need experience to be good at it, you just need the ingredients for the dish and instructions on how to prepare it.
